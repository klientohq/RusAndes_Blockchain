CEO PHOTO INSTRUCTIONS
======================

1. Name your photo file exactly:  ceo-photo.jpg
2. Place it in this folder:        assets/ceo-photo.jpg
3. That's it — the website will show it automatically.

Recommended specs:
- Format: JPG or WebP
- Size: 600x700 px minimum (portrait orientation)
- Subject: centered, upper body / head shot
- Background: dark or neutral works best with the site design

The photo slot is in the About section. If the file is missing,
the "AFP" gold initials show as a fallback — nothing breaks.
